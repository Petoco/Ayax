User Guide

1. Invitation

Go to https://ayax-xyz.glitch.me/invite and select the server and authorize the Ayax basic permissions.

ㅤ

ㅤ

2. Using Ayax

Connect into a voice channel and write the command /play [song] or .play [song] .

ㅤ

ㅤ

3. Listen the song/playlist

Ayax will connect into your voice channel and start to play the song or the playlist you sent.
